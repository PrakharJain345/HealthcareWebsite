Our Services

Cardiology

  • Advanced Diagnostic Imaging: State-of-the-art imaging technologies like 3D echocardiography, cardiac MRI, and CT angiography for precise diagnosis of cardiovascular conditions.
  • Electrophysiology Lab: Equipped for detailed assessment and treatment of heart rhythm disorders, including ablation procedures and pacemaker/ICD implantation.
  • 24/7 Cardiac Care Unit: A dedicated unit staffed with specialized cardiologists and nurses for continuous monitoring and emergency care.

Orthopaedics and Joint Replacement

  • Joint Replacement Center: Specialized program for total and partial joint replacements including pre-operative planning, advanced surgical techniques, and post-operative rehabilitation.
  • Sports Medicine Clinic: Dedicated care for athletes and active individuals, offering treatments for sports-related injuries, rehabilitation programs, and performance enhancement.
  • Orthopaedic Research and Clinical Trials: Access to the latest advancements in orthopaedic medicine through participation in research studies and clinical trials, offering patients cutting-edge treatments and therapies.

Neurology

  • Advanced Neuroimaging: Cutting-edge MRI, PET scans, and functional MRI for detailed brain imaging to diagnose and monitor neurological conditions.
  • Epilepsy Monitoring Unit (EMU): Dedicated unit for the comprehensive evaluation of epilepsy, including continuous EEG monitoring to refine diagnosis and treatment.
  • Stroke Care Unit: A specialized unit focused on the rapid diagnosis and treatment of stroke, including thrombolysis and thrombectomy procedures.
